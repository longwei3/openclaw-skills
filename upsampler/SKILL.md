# Upsampler Video Generation

Generate videos for free using Upsampler's AI video tools. No signup required, no watermark.

## Usage

### Web Interface

1. Visit: https://upsampler.com/free-video-generator-no-signup
2. Choose model (Wan 2.2 5B, Wan 2.2 14B, Wan 2.2 14B Pro, LTX 2 Distilled Turbo)
3. Enter text prompt or upload an image
4. Select aspect ratio (Landscape 16:9, Portrait 9:16, Square 1:1)
5. Click "Generate Video"
6. Wait for generation (usually 1-3 minutes)
7. Click "Download" to save the video

### Features

- **Text to Video**: Describe the video you want
- **Image to Video**: Animate static images
- **Multiple Models**: Wan 2.2 series, LTX 2 Distilled Turbo
- **Free & No Watermark**: Unlimited generations, no signup needed

## Tips

1. **Be specific** - Include details about motion, lighting, and style
2. **Start simple** - Complex scenes may not render well
3. **Try different models** - Different models produce different results
4. **Image to video** - Upload a reference image for better results
